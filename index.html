desire lines

album cover, single cover

This book aims to get the user to implement the values of Wabi-Sabi into their lives by taking example in something we all know and can relate to. A traditional coffee table book is big, well crafted and typically not meant to be read in one sitting, making it kind of at odds with its purpose. This book is big, well crafted but only takes 5-10 minutes to complete. This way it fits much better in at the places you usually find coffee table books. This book was my final project during my first year in the Communication Design program at Designskolen Kolding. After the examination, the administration bought 2 copies of the book to be shown on the halls of the school. I refined the screen printed graphic elements on the cover and inside the book.

info / archive

Bjarki Björnsson is a Copenhagen-based creative and curator known for his distinct aesthetic that bridges the worlds of underground music, fashion, and digital archiving. Through his various platforms, he functions as a digital tastemaker, sharing a mix of obscure visual media, nostalgic film photography, and eclectic soundscapes. His work often focuses on preserving internet subcultures and promoting alternative media, making him a central figure for those interested in high-concept style and niche cultural discovery.
