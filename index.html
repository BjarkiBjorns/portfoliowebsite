Desire Lines

photo, composition, layout, bookbinding, screenprinting, concept

2024

This book aims to get the user to implement the values of Wabi-Sabi into their lives by taking example in something we all know and can relate to. A traditional coffee table book is big, well crafted and typically not meant to be read in one sitting, making it kind of at odds with its purpose. This book is big, well crafted but only takes 5-10 minutes to complete. This way it fits much better in at the places you usually find coffee table books.

This book was my final project during my first year in the Communication Design program at Designskolen Kolding. After the examination, the administration bought 2 copies of the book to be shown on the halls of the school.

I refined the screen printed graphic elements on the cover and inside the book.

Aarhus University

2025

This is an interactive mini designguide I made for Aarhus University. The focus was on balancing themes of collaboration and playfulness with “serious institution” in the visual language.

The designguide was designed specially to be easy to navigate. From the table of contents, you are able to navigate to any chapter and back by simply clicking a title.
